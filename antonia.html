Antonia

Detalii Wikipedia:

Antonia Clara Iacobescu (born 12 April 1989), commonly known as Antonia, is a Romanian-American singer, performer, and model. In 2017 the artist was chosen by 20th Century Fox to provide the Romanian dub voice of Jeanette for the movie Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Road Chip.
